ABOUT US

Border collies originate form the hilly farming areas that form the border regions of England with both Scotland and Wales; these rugged landscapes saw the necessity for an all pupose stock and herding dog form which the border collie was born. Intelligence and tenacity,speed and agility and the ability to work tirelessly form morning until night were the valued assets in these dogs and form the basic characteristics of the breed.

WHAT WE DO

We breed working Border Collies with care and a special attention given to the genetic and hereditary traits. we find important to produce a litter when we have extra time.

WHY WE DO IT

Border Collies are the dogs we breed,the dogs we love and the dogs we devote our life to. we have the improvement of this unique breed at heart and try to give birth to the most amazing working line ever.

HOW WE DO IT

Only our best dogs are bred and, helped by vets and professionals, we handpick their partners from other successful and famous bloodlines in order to equip the litters with a wonderful genetic variation.
